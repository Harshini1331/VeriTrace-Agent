DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM (DPA)

This Addendum is entered into by and between [Company Name] ("Controller") and [Partner Name] ("Processor").

1. DATA PROTECTION
1.1. The Processor agrees to process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Controller.
1.2. The Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security, confidentiality, and integrity of Personal Data.

2. SUB-PROCESSORS
2.1. The Processor shall not engage any sub-processor without prior written authorization from the Controller.

3. AUDIT RIGHTS
3.1. The Controller has the right to audit the Processor's compliance with this Addendum at least once annually.
